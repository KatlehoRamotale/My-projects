Hotel Overview

Grand Palace Hotel is a modern hospitality destination designed to provide guests with comfort, luxury, and convenience. Our hotel offers a range of beautifully prepared rooms, including Single Rooms, Double Rooms, and Suites, each designed to meet different guest needs and budgets.

The hotel management website allows guests to explore available rooms, view prices, make bookings, and manage their reservations easily. Through the system, users can register, log in, select their preferred room, choose check-in and check-out dates, and confirm bookings in a simple and professional way.

For management purposes, the system also includes booking dashboards, reservation tracking, room availability checks, payment simulation, and profile management. Admin users are able to monitor all bookings, manage customer reservations, and oversee hotel operations from one place.

At Grand Palace Hotel, our goal is to combine luxury accommodation with smart digital convenience, creating a smooth and efficient experience for both guests and hotel staff.
